### Demo Video: `emergency_demo.mp4`

**Location:** `demo/` folder

**Description:**
This video showcases the **TherapyX AI Mental Health Assistant** handling high-risk messages in real time. It highlights the system’s emergency response capabilities, including detection, notification, and alerting.

**Video Highlights:**

1. **User Interaction with the AI:**

   * A user types a harmful message into the Streamlit interface.

2. **AI Response:**

   * The AI analyzes the message and identifies it as **high-risk**.
   * Emergency tools in the backend are triggered automatically.

3. **Notifications and Alerts:**

   * A **WhatsApp message** is sent instantly to the predefined emergency contact.
   * An **automatic phone call** is placed to the emergency contact’s number.

**What You’ll See in the Video:**

* Your phone receiving the WhatsApp notification.
* Incoming call from the AI system’s emergency number.
* Streamlit interface showing the AI detecting and responding to the harmful message in real time.

**Purpose:**
This video demonstrates the **real-time emergency response functionality** of TherapyX, proving its ability to **detect high-risk messages** and **notify designated contacts automatically**, ensuring timely intervention.
